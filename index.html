⚠️ Important Rules

  • Gender (Page 2): Words ending in a taa marbuta (ة) are feminine. Words without it are masculine.
  • "This is..." (Page 5): Use هَذَا for masculine nouns and هَذِهِ for feminine nouns.
  • Adjectives (Page 7): Adjectives must come after the noun. If the noun is feminine (ends in ة), the adjective must also be feminine (add ة).
  • "And" (Page 8): The word for "and" is وَ.
  • Questions (Pages 9 & 14): هَلْ means "Is this...?", نَعَمْ means "Yes", لَا means "No", and أَمْ means "or".

1️⃣ Translations

🐱 This is a small cat
Reveal Answer
هَذِهِ قِطَّةٌ صَغِيرَةٌ
WHY Cat is قِطَّةٌ (page 11). It ends in ة so it is feminine. We use هَذِهِ (This is - fem). The adjective small is صَغِيرَةٌ (page 7) and comes after the noun.
🦋 This is a light butterfly
Reveal Answer
هَذِهِ فَرَاشَةٌ خَفِيفَةٌ
WHY Butterfly is فَرَاشَةٌ (page 11). Feminine noun + هَذِهِ + feminine adjective for light خَفِيفَةٌ (page 7).
⭐ This is a new star
Reveal Answer
هَذِهِ نَجْمَةٌ جَدِيدَةٌ
WHY Star is نَجْمَةٌ (page 13). Feminine noun + هَذِهِ + feminine adjective for new جَدِيدَةٌ (page 7).
❤️ This is a small heart
Reveal Answer
هَذَا قَلْبٌ صَغِيرٌ
WHY Heart is قَلْبٌ (page 13). It does not end in ة, so it is masculine. We use هَذَا (This is - masc) + the masculine adjective for small صَغِيرٌ (page 7).

🏚️ This is an old house and this is a new house
Reveal Answer
هَذَا بَيْتٌ قَدِيمٌ وَهَذَا بَيْتٌ جَدِيدٌ
WHY House (بَيْتٌ) is masculine (page 4). Old is قَدِيمٌ and new is جَدِيدٌ (page 7). We connect them with وَ (and).
✏️ This is a small pencil and this is a big pencil
Reveal Answer
هَذَا قَلَمٌ صَغِيرٌ وَهَذَا قَلَمٌ كَبِيرٌ
WHY Pen/Pencil is قَلَمٌ (masculine, page 3). Small is صَغِيرٌ and big is كَبِيرٌ.

3️⃣ Shapes & "Or" Questions

🔺 Is this a triangle or heart? (هَلْ هَذَا مُثَلَّثٌ أَمْ قَلْبٌ؟)
Reveal Answer
هَذَا مُثَلَّثٌ
WHY Vocab is from page 13. أَمْ means "or" (page 14). The picture is a triangle.
⭕ Is this a star or circle? (هَلْ هَذِهِ نَجْمَةٌ أَمْ دَائِرَةٌ؟)
Reveal Answer
هَذِهِ دَائِرَةٌ
